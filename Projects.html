Project - AWS Home Made Pickles


▼ Project Introduction

The Homemade Pickle Project is a small-scale food business initiative focused on producing and selling traditional homemade pickles. The project aims to provide customers with fresh, hygienic, and preservative-free pickles made from natural ingredients and authentic recipes.

Features:

  • Mango pickle catalog
  • Lemon pickle catalog
  • Shopping cart
  • Customer login
  • Admin dashboard for inventory management

Challenges:

  • Produce high-quality homemade pickles.
  • Preserve traditional flavors and recipes.

Technologies Used:

Python, Flask, AWS EC2, DynamoDB, HTML, CSS
